b(θ)
DERIVATIVE PROCESSOR
∂ b(θ) / ∂θ
∂² b(θ) / ∂θ²
μ = b'(θ)
Var(y) = ϕ · b''(θ)

Misconception Corrected

Mean and variance are internally derived from the curvature of b(θ). This corrects the misconception that mean and variance are always independent parameters in GLMs. In the exponential family, the variance is functionally tied to the mean.
