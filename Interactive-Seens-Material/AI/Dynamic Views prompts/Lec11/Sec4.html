Misconception Corrected

The difference in deviances acts as a log-likelihood ratio statistic mapped to a χ² distribution. This corrects the misconception that hierarchical model selection is independent of the log-likelihood ratio testing framework. Partitioning deviance is directly equivalent to LR testing.
