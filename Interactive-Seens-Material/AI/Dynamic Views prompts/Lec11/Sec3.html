Misconception Corrected

The weighted residuals accumulate into the Pearson χ² statistic. This corrects the misconception that deviance is the sole mathematical framework for measuring GLM discrepancy. Pearson χ² is an alternative residual-based goodness-of-fit metric equivalent to deviance in large samples.
