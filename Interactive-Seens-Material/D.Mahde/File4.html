The Hardware Misconception

It is a misconception that faster speed is achieved with simpler hardware. The CLA completely eliminates linear delay by drastically increasing gate complexity. The central LCU uses massive, highly complex AND/OR gates to compute all carry values simultaneously, trading silicon area for parallel speed.
