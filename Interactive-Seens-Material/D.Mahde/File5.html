ALU Process

Dynamic Reconfiguration

Interact with the ALUControl selector to change how the Arithmetic Logic Unit processes the static inputs A=5 and B=3.

ALUControl Selector

The ALUControl is 00. The Multiplexer connects the AND Logic output to the final Result. Note that all other blocks are still physically computing, but their results are ignored.

The Grand Misconception

It is commonly thought that processors require separate, standalone physical circuits for every distinct logical command. In reality, an ALU is a unified multi-functional unit. It executes dozens of instructions by using Multiplexers to reconfigure internal data routing, drastically saving physical space.
