💡 How to Read this Visualization

1. Drag Horizontal (Real Part, -a)

Moving poles left/right adjusts damping (a).
• Left of axis (a > 0): Stable decay.
• Right of axis (a < 0): Unstable exponential expansion.
• On vertical axis (a = 0): Undamped perpetual oscillation.

2. Drag Vertical (Imaginary Part, ω)

Moving poles up/down adjusts oscillation speed (ω).
• Higher ω squeezes cycles closer together (shorter period T).
• Pulling poles onto the horizontal line eliminates oscillation completely (DC / Pure exponential response).

3. Mathematical Link

Every dynamic system is defined by its denominator. Completing the square:
s² + 2as + (a² + ω²) = (s+a)² + ω² This shape directly corresponds to the time envelope e⁻ᵃᵗ and sinusoid rate sin(ωt).
