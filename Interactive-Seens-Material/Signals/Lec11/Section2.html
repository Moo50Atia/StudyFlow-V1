Transfer Function H(z)

Visualizing the transformation from a discrete Time-Domain Difference Equation to a Z-Domain Transfer Function.

1. Time Domain

Start with the recursion difference equation representing the discrete-time system.

2. Apply Z-Transform

Time-delay operators (y[n-k]) directly transform into algebraic delay blocks (z-k) attached to Y(z).

3. Factor Outputs & Inputs

Factor out the common Y(z) terms on the left, and X(z) terms on the right.

4. Isolate H(z)

Rearrange the algebraic blocks to solve for the ratio Y(z) / X(z), resulting in the rational Transfer Function H(z).
